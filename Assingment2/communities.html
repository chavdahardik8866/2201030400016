Joshua Vincente
Cachoeirinha, Brazil
As the community manager for Cachoeirinha, Brazil, I act as a bridge between our community and the rest of the world. This ranges from connecting community members with like-minded people around the world, welcoming new residents/visitors to our community, listening to feedback, answering questions, and addressing concerns. I also organize regular town hall meetings, surveys, and community forums to involve residents in decision-making processes and gather their input on priorities. Please feel free to reach out and connect! I am fluent in english, portuguese, and spanish.

We Are Always Searching For More Community Managers To Add To Our Team!

Community management lies at the heart of our organization. They are the thread that weaves together our worldwide network of community members, supporters, volunteers, donors, and advocates. Our community managers play a pivotal role in fostering meaningful connections, building partnerships, and ensuring that our shared values resonate across borders. If you are inspired to use your skills and passion for community building to drive positive change, we invite you to apply for our community manager position.
